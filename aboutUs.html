Who we are


INDLAB TOOLS was formed in the year 1987 as a partnership Firm with a view to serve the Scientific Industry and cater the needs of R & D and QC Laboratories in India.


INDLAB TOOLS has got its office in Ashok Nagar, a fast developing part of Chennai.


From Chennai we have got easy mode of conveyance to reach any part of India.


INDLAB TOOLS has got more than 25 years experience in the field of marketing scientific instruments Laboratory Furnishing.


INDLABTOOLS with its experienced marketing net work is concentrating on Agriculture, Automobile Industries, Food Industries and Medical & Health industries. Presently we concentrate in Tamil Nadu, our home state, and are also catering the needs of our customers in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Karnataka, Goa and Pondicherry.


Besides direct customer contacts, we enjoy good dealer network to take care of customers need wherever required.


We have a good amount of satisfied customers, who also refer us to new customers and also support us by placing repeat orders for their new projects.


We keep on trying to expand our customer base and serve the Scientific and Industrial sectors by providing quality products at reasonable cost with efficient Service support.
